Welcome to CodeProb!

We are thrilled to have you here. Whether you're a seasoned programmer or just starting your coding journey, CodeProb is a vibrant community-driven platform dedicated to making programming knowledge accessible, organized, and inspiring for everyone.

What is CodeProb?

CodeProb is a Wikipedia-inspired, static website that combines focused coding problems with educational content. Our mission is to provide a clean, minimal, and content-focused environment where learners and contributors can come together to share, discover, and grow.

For Contributors

Join our community and help shape the future of programming education!

  • Create: Use the Writer Interface to craft problems, concepts, or articles.
  • Export: Download your content as HTML files.
  • Submit: Fork the repository, add your files, and create a pull request.
  • Review & Merge: Collaborate with community members and maintainers to bring your contributions to life.

    • For Users

    Explore, learn, practice, and contribute!

  • Browse: Navigate through diverse content categories.
  • Learn: Dive into concepts and detailed articles.
  • Practice: Solve engaging programming problems.
  • Contribute: Share your own knowledge and insights with the community.

    • Our Visual Identity

  • Inspired by Wikipedia: Clean, minimal, and content-focused.
  • Dual themes: Light and dark mode for comfortable reading.
  • Typography: Serif for content, sans-serif for interface.
  • Colors: Neutral palette with vibrant blue accents.
  • Layout: Centered content with spacious margins for readability.

    • Our Commitment to Quality & Accessibility

  • Works seamlessly without JavaScript.
  • Mobile-first, responsive design.
  • Screen reader friendly and keyboard navigable.
  • Fast loading and optimized performance.

Join Our Community!

Your contributions, questions, and feedback are what make CodeProb a thriving space for learning and sharing. Whether you're creating content, reviewing submissions, or engaging in discussions, you play a vital role in building a better programming education platform.
